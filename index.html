Hi! My name is Shea Edwards and I am a first year graduate student in the Learning Design and Technology program at NC State!

My Favorite edTech Tools:

  • Flipgrid- this tool allows for discussion between students using video. This can be more engaging and interactive for students than a normal discussion forum
  • Remind- this tool was used by one of my high school instructors to send out reminders to students phones without using her personal number. I found it to be a very professional and effective way for instructors to share information.
  • Padlet- this tool allows for interactive boards that students can share ideas on, or it can be used as a personal board for students to post progress for their projects and assignments
